About me

Hello, my name is Marie. I am originally from Paris, but I currently live in Zurich and I am planning to move in London. As a matter of fact, I prefer to live in a English speaking country, and close to my family
I graduated with a health care management master degree in 2015 and I worked in this field until 2019. I worked in hospital, nursing home, schizophrenic helping structure and intellectual disability helping structure.
In 2019, I moved to Switzerland. At that time, computer science was just a hobby, then I tried to improve my skills in JavaScript to transform this passion into a job.

Why am I applying to this programme ?

When I began to learn HTML and CSS, it was just by curiosity, but I liked it so I imagined that I could do my own website. I have a few ideas to develop, but I still miss some skills before being ready to do so.
This programme could help me to improve my skills, not to just develop a website, but to be part of a bigger project by being in a company and then maybe create mine.
When I was working in health care, we (the whole health care system) were always struggling with the software limitations. Today I can see how easy the problems could be resolved and I wonder how I can help the health care providers.
